About this map

This map was built for the purpose of being a helpful tool for the user to find their ideal location for stargazing

Most people live under heavy artificial light which makes it helpful to find dark sky zones, urban areas to avoid, and sky brightness

Here are the data sources used for building this map

  1. National parks boundary
  2. USGS protected areas database
  3. Urban areas – united states (polygon layer)
  4. NASA/ NOAA VIIRS nighttime lights – Global coverage (updated yearly)
